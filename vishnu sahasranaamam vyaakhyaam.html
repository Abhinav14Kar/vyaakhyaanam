yat granthavali pradeshini

Aum sri gurubhyo namah

Sri Chaitanya manobhishtham sthapitam yenah bhootale swayam roopam kada mahiyam
dadathi nijh padantikam

Atha Sri Vishnu Sahasranaamam

Aum viswam vishnor vashatkaaro bhoot bhavya bhavad prabhoh
bhootah krid bhoot bhrid bhaavoh bhhotatmam bhootah bhavanah


yasya dwo prathamah panktim sriman narayana dwiya viswam vishnu vashatkaram cha
naamam sambandah

sri vishnu sahasranaamam prathamah shlok vyaakhya
  • Sri Harir divya naamam:-
    1. viswam
    2. vishno
    3. vashatkaaro
    4. bhootah bhavya bhavat prabhuh
    5. bhootah krit
    6. bhootah bhrid
    7. bhavah
    8. bhhotatmah
    9. bhooth bhavanah
    Yasmin viswam artham sarva vishwanaam yat aadharam sa vishwam. sarvamidam proktaam yenah yatha vishno
    sarva yagnam parat roopam ya vashatkaaram asit.ya devam prabhuh bhootam vartaman tatha bhavishyam, ta vai bhootah bhvyad bhavat prabhuh. Pancha mahabhootam kritam yenah tah vai bhootah krit.
    shrutir vaakhyam,"vasudev sarvam iti" artham bhaavo yasmin sri harim astu. sarvaatmane yenah ta vai bhootatmah. sarva jeevasya jeevan chakraadharam yasya ta vai bhoot bhavanah.
